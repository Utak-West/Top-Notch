Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Pedro Ribeiro, Top Notch Renovations & Home Services was born from a passion for quality craftsmanship and a vision to provide integrated renovation and electrical services under one roof.

After spending over a decade working for various construction and electrical companies throughout New Jersey, Pedro recognized a common frustration among homeowners: the disconnect between renovation contractors and electrical specialists often led to project delays, communication issues, and inconsistent quality.

Drawing on his dual expertise in both fields, Pedro established Top Notch with a simple mission: to deliver seamless, high-quality home transformations by bringing renovation and electrical services together under one cohesive team.

Our Mission

To transform homes with quality craftsmanship, integrated expertise, and exceptional customer service that exceeds expectations and creates spaces our clients love to live in.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in every aspect of our business.
  • Quality: We never compromise on materials or workmanship.
  • Reliability: We honor our commitments with consistent, dependable service.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our methods and stay current with industry trends.
