Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Linden, NJ and the surrounding areas in Union County, including Elizabeth, Rahway, Cranford, Westfield, Clark, Roselle, Union, Scotch Plains, Fanwood, Springfield, and Summit.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Top Notch Renovations & Home Services is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of New Jersey. We maintain all necessary credentials for renovation and electrical work.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A bathroom renovation may take 2-3 weeks, while a complete kitchen remodel typically requires 4-6 weeks. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free consultations and estimates for all projects. This allows us to understand your needs, assess the space, and provide an accurate quote before any work begins.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate required inspections as part of our service, ensuring your project is fully compliant with local building codes.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept checks, credit cards, and electronic transfers. For larger projects, we typically work with a payment schedule tied to project milestones.
