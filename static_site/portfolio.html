Modern Kitchen Renovation

Location

Linden, NJ

Project Type

Kitchen Renovation

Duration

5 Weeks

Project Overview

This modern kitchen renovation transformed an outdated 1990s kitchen into a sleek, functional space for a family of four. The clients wanted an open concept design with improved flow, more storage, and updated appliances.

Scope of Work

  • Complete demolition of existing kitchen
  • Removal of wall between kitchen and dining area
  • New custom cabinetry with soft-close drawers
  • Quartz countertops and marble backsplash
  • New energy-efficient stainless steel appliances
  • Center island with seating and storage
  • Recessed, pendant, and under-cabinet lighting
  • New hardwood flooring to match adjacent rooms
  • Fresh paint throughout

Challenges & Solutions

During demolition, we discovered outdated electrical wiring that didn't meet current code requirements. Our integrated expertise allowed us to handle both the renovation and electrical work seamlessly, bringing everything up to code without delays or the need for a separate contractor.

Results

The finished kitchen provides a beautiful, functional space that meets all the family's needs while significantly increasing the home's value. The clients were thrilled with both the quality of work and how we managed to complete the project on time despite the unexpected electrical issues.

"Pedro and his team transformed our outdated kitchen into a beautiful, functional space that has become the heart of our home. Their attention to detail and problem-solving abilities were impressive, especially when they discovered and fixed electrical issues we didn't even know existed."
- The Johnson Family
