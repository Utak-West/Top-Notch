Our Services

Comprehensive renovation and electrical services for your home improvement needs

At Top Notch Renovations & Home Services, we provide integrated expertise in both renovation and electrical work. This unique combination allows us to manage complex projects efficiently, eliminating the need to coordinate between multiple contractors and ensuring seamless execution from start to finish.

Complete Renovations

Transform your living spaces with our comprehensive renovation services. We handle everything from concept to completion, delivering beautiful, functional spaces that reflect your vision and lifestyle. Our integrated expertise in both construction and electrical work ensures that all systems work harmoniously within your newly renovated space.

  • Kitchen Renovations: Custom cabinets, countertops, islands, backsplashes, lighting, and appliance installation.
  • Bathroom Renovations: Tubs, showers, vanities, toilets, lighting, and ventilation.
  • Basement Finishing: Creating functional living spaces with proper insulation, lighting, and ventilation.
  • Room Additions: Expanding your home's footprint and living space.
  • Interior Remodeling: Reconfiguring layouts for improved flow and functionality.

  • Wiring & Rewiring: New installations and updates for older homes with outdated wiring.
